\keywords{service level agreement (SLA), data integration, services, cloud, smart energy}

\abstract{
This paper proposes data integration  (lookup, aggregation, correlation) strategies adapted to the vision of the economic model of the cloud such as accepting partial results delivered on demand or under  subscription models that can affect the quality of the results; accepting specific data duplication that can respect privacy but ensure data availability; accepting to launch a task that contributes to an integration on a first cloud whose SLA verifies security requirement rather that a more powerful cloud but with less security guarantees in the SLA. 
}
